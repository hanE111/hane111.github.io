About

I am an undergraduate student in Computer Science & Biology, with research experience in digital mental health, molecular modeling, and drug response prediction. My long-term goal is to pursue a PhD and lead a research group that develops interpretable machine learning systems to improve how we prevent, diagnose, and treat disease.

I particularly enjoy working on problems that connect rigorous modeling with clinically meaningful questions: from understanding behavioral digital biomarkers to interpreting how molecular structure and multi-omics features relate to therapeutic response.

Research Interests

Biomedical AI

Interpretable machine learning models for precision medicine, clinical decision support, and patient-specific outcome prediction.

Drug Discovery & Molecular Modeling

Molecular graph–language models, 3D-aware representation learning, and captioning models that connect chemical structure to human-readable explanations.

Digital Biomarkers & Mental Health

Passive sensing, behavioral time-series analysis, and feature selection methods to derive robust digital biomarkers from real-world data.
